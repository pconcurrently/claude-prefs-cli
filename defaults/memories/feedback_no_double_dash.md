---
name: No double dashes
description: Use single dash (-) instead of double dash (--) as separators in text
type: feedback
---

Use single dash (-) instead of double dash (--) as a separator in all output, including plan descriptions, roadmap entries, and general text.

**Why:** User preference for cleaner formatting.
**How to apply:** Whenever writing descriptions, separators, or inline dividers, use ` - ` not ` -- `.
