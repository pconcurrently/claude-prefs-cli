Do not use em dashes (—) or double dashes (--) anywhere - not in prose, commits, comments, or any written output.

**Why:** User dislikes em dashes and double dashes as a formatting choice.

**How to apply:** Use a single regular dash (-) as the replacement. Never use -- (double dash) as a substitute for em dash. One dash only.
